Modified Interest Subvention Scheme
(MISS)

Initiatives by Central Ministries and Departments

Overview

The Modified Interest Subvention Scheme (MISS), originally conceived as the Rythu Bandhu Scheme by the Government of Telangana, is a landmark initiative designed to provide direct financial support to eligible farmers. Under this scheme, a specific amount is given directly to farmers to assist with agricultural investments, offering a more sustainable alternative to traditional farm loan waivers.

Key Features of MISS (Rythu Bandhu Scheme):
1. Direct Financial Support
2. Successful Implementation and Recognition
3. National Adoption

Ministry of Agriculture and Information Technology

Eligibility Criteria:-

  • Small and marginal farmers owning up to 2 hectares (about 5 acres) of cultivable land.
  • Land ownership records must be maintained by the state/UT government.
  • Farmers must be registered under the scheme and have an Aadhaar card for identification.

Registration Process:-

  • Offline: Farmers can register through their local agriculture office or Common Service Centers (CSCs).
  • Online: Registration can also be done via the official PM-Kisan portal (pmkisan.gov.in) .
  • Documents Required:
    • Aadhaar number
    • Bank account details
    • Land ownership documents

Benefits:-

  • Financial Support: Provides a steady source of income for farmers to support crop investment and other agricultural activities.
  • Poverty Alleviation: Aims to reduce poverty among small and marginal farmers by providing financial stability.
  • Improving Agricultural Productivity: Enables farmers to invest in better seeds, fertilizers, and other inputs, potentially increasing productivity.

Recent Updates:-

  • The government periodically updates the list of beneficiaries and conducts verification to ensure eligibility.
  • The scheme's scope and reach are reviewed regularly to address gaps or issues in implementation.

How to Check Beneficiary Status:-

Farmers can check their beneficiary status on the official PM-Kisan website by entering their Aadhaar number, bank account number, or registered mobile number: pmkisan.gov.in.
