Agriculture Infrastructure Fund
(AIS)

Initiatives by Central Ministries and Departments

Overview

The Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF) is a financing facility launched by the Government of India to support the creation of post-harvest management infrastructure and community farming assets. This scheme aims to provide medium to long-term debt financing for investment in viable projects for post-harvest management infrastructure and community farming assets through interest subvention and credit guarantee. The scheme will support farmers, primary agricultural credit societies (PACS), marketing cooperative societies, farmer producer organizations (FPOs), self-help groups (SHGs), and agri-entrepreneurs.

The Fund will catalyze the creation of post-harvest management infrastructure such as cold storage, collection centers, processing units, and ripening chambers, which will enable farmers to earn better prices for their produce, reduce wastage, and improve the overall value chain.

Eligibility Criteria:-

  • Farmers, Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs), PACS, SHGs, agri-entrepreneurs, start-ups, and cooperatives.
  • Entities involved in post-harvest management projects or community farming assets.
  • Farmers must be registered under the scheme and have an Aadhaar card for identification.

Financial Support:

  • Interest subvention of 3% per annum on all loans up to Rs. 2 crore.
  • Credit guarantee coverage for loans up to Rs. 2 crore under Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE).
  • Loans to be repaid in a period of up to 7 years.

Registration Process:-

  • Interested parties can apply through the bank branches, financial institutions, or the official Agriculture Infrastructure Fund portal.
  • Documents Required: Proof of identity, project proposal, financial details, etc.

Benefits:-

  • Facilitates the development of agri-infrastructure projects that will help farmers store and sell their produce in better conditions.
  • Promotes job creation in rural areas.
  • Encourages the adoption of modern technology and methods in farming.

Recent Updates:-

  • The government regularly reviews the progress of the scheme and its impact on the agricultural sector.
  • New guidelines and criteria are introduced to ensure effective implementation and wider reach.

How to Check Application Status:-

Applicants can check the status of their applications on the Agriculture Infrastructure Fund portal or through the participating banks or financial institutions.
